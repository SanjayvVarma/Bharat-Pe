BharatPe was started in 2018 with the mission to build a holistic FinTech platform for offline merchants and small businesses. Today, BharatPe offers a suite of simple, easy-to-use and secure fintech products including payments and business loans to its merchant partners. The company has an extensive network of 10 million merchants across 400+ cities in the country. BharatPe’s consumer offering postpe is one of the fastest-growing products in the Buy-Now- Pay-Later industry.
